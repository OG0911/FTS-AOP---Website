SINCE 2024, REINVENTING DA KASI WE LOVE TO WHAT WE LOVE

Welcome to From the South, a vibrant and proudly South African roadhouse founded in 2024, nestled in the bustling city of Pretoria. Born out of a love for local cuisine and a desire to bring people together, we’ve crafted a unique dining experience that reflects the rich diversity and flavor of South Africa.

At From the South, we’re passionate about serving up an array of delicious meals that satisfy every palate. Our menu is a celebration of South African culture, offering everything from traditional roadhouse classics to homegrown favorites. We proudly specialize in serving iconic dishes like hearty Dagwoods stacked with layers of flavor, mouthwatering kotas bursting with fillings, and the legendary Gatsbys that are big enough to share.

Our food is made with quality ingredients and prepared with care, delivering fresh, flavorful meals every time. Whether you're stopping by for a quick takeaway or enjoying a meal with family and friends, From the South promises to leave you satisfied and coming back for more. But it’s not just about the food—it's about the experience. Our roadhouse brings a nostalgic feel with a modern twist, blending old-school charm with contemporary convenience. You can pull up in your car, place your order, and enjoy your meal from the comfort of your vehicle, or grab a table and savor the atmosphere.

As a proudly local establishment, we take pride in supporting the community and serving food that truly represents South Africa’s culinary heritage. From our flavorful Dagwoods to our epic kotas and legendary Gatsbys, every dish is a tribute to the tastes that make South Africa special. Come visit us at From the South—where great food, local flavor, and the spirit of Pretoria come together in one delicious place!
